About Me
I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at Indiana University Bloomington, specializing in behavioral macroeconomics. My research focuses on the role of news media in shaping household inflation expectations and how incentives influence forecasting behavior. I employ machine learning techniques, textual analysis, and survey experiments in my work.

I am on the 2025-2026 academic job market. ​I will present at the 2026 AEA/ASSA Annual Meeting in Philadelphia and would be happy to meet and discuss research. Feel free to reach out at sdrobot@iu.edu.
